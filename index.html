Business Sector

We bridge the gap between your Requirement Needs, Budget, and Acquisition Planning Process!

IAS provides management and insight on your entire requirement process from inception to closeout which enables decision-makers on all levels to be more informed about the organization’s needs and how resources are allocated. Thus, ensuring better financial decisions are made to achieve the organization’s mission objectives.

We accomplish this by utilizing our own in-house application, the Resource Management and Decision-Making Tool (RMDT ™). The RMDT ™ provides insight on how best to Plan, Execute, Monitor, Report, Assess, Decide-On and Adjust how resources are allocated throughout an organization. The RMDT ™ maps and links your organization’s Requirements, Budget, Acquisition Planning, Contract Management, and Closeout functions. It serves as a Requirements Hub to provide processing status and data analytics information so that your organization’s full financial picture can be seen allin one place when making resource allocation decisions.
